# Acme R2 Launch Announcement

Acme Robotics is pleased to announce the upcoming launch of the Acme R2, the next generation of our mobile manipulation platform.

**What's new in R2:**

- New 7-DoF arm with a rated payload of 12 kg
- Redesigned lithium-iron-phosphate battery, 10-hour runtime
- Hot-swappable tool interface
- On-board NPU for local inference (20 TOPS)
- Full ROS 2 Jazzy support at launch

The R2 maintains software compatibility with existing R1 SDK integrations. Pre-orders open next month; shipping begins early next year.
