# Photosynthesis — Study Notes (draft)

These are informal study notes. Some of the details below are wrong and should be corrected against a textbook.

- Photosynthesis happens in chloroplasts. Good.
- Light reactions: occur in the stroma (the fluid-filled region of the chloroplast). They produce ATP and NADPH.
- Calvin cycle: occurs in the thylakoid membrane and fixes CO2.
- Chlorophyll absorbs green light most efficiently, which is why it appears green.
