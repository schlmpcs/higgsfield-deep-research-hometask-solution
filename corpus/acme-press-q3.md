# Acme Robotics — Q3 Press Release

Acme Robotics today announced its Q3 earnings and shared upcoming product plans.

The flagship Acme R1 platform saw strong adoption in the logistics sector this quarter. A firmware update released in August boosted the R1's rated arm payload to **7 kg**, up from the previous specification, after extensive field testing on heavier SKUs in warehouse deployments. (This figure supersedes earlier datasheets.)

Acme also previewed the R2, scheduled for launch next quarter, which will introduce a redesigned chassis and an improved battery system.

"We're seeing real traction in logistics and light manufacturing," said CEO Priya Raman. "Our customers have been asking for heavier payload handling, and we delivered."
