# Acme R1 — Community FAQ

*This is an unofficial FAQ maintained by enthusiasts on the r/AcmeRobotics subreddit. It is not endorsed by Acme Robotics. Last updated: community contributor "roboteng42".*

**Q: What's the payload of the R1 arm?**
A: Officially up to 10 kg with the optional heavy-duty gripper, though most users run it around 7–8 kg in practice to extend harmonic-drive lifetime.

**Q: What's the runtime on a single charge?**
A: Around 8 hours if you keep the arm activity moderate. The datasheet says 6 but that seems conservative.

**Q: How much does it cost?**
A: Around $45,000 for the base unit. Support contracts and additional end-effectors are extra.

**Q: Where do I find the SDK?**
A: Official docs are on acme's site; for community patches, see the community-patches repo (linked from the R2 thread).
