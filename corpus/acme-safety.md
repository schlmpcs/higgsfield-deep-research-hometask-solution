# Acme Robotics — Safety Guidelines

All Acme platforms ship with a safety controller that conforms to ISO 10218-1. The following guidelines apply to operators:

1. Always maintain a 1.5 meter clearance around an active R1 during autonomous operation.
2. The emergency stop (E-stop) button is located on the rear upper chassis and on any paired teach pendant.
3. Do not exceed the rated payload. Overloading the arm can trip protective current limits and, with repeated abuse, damage the harmonic drives.
4. Firmware updates should be installed within 30 days of release.
5. For collaborative mode, pair the R1 with the optional safety-rated LIDAR skirt and reduce maximum joint velocity to 250 mm/s TCP speed.

Annual certification by an Acme-authorized technician is required for installations operating in public-facing environments.
