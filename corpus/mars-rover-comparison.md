# NASA Mars Rovers — A Quick Comparison

A summary of the two active NASA rovers on Mars:

| Rover | Landed | Landing site | Power |
|-------|--------|--------------|-------|
| Curiosity | 2012 | Gale Crater | Solar panels |
| Perseverance | 2021 | Jezero Crater | Solar panels |

Both rovers are roughly the size of a small car and use a similar chassis. Perseverance adds a sample-caching system and carried the Ingenuity helicopter technology demonstrator, which completed the first powered flight on another planet.

Both missions are part of NASA's ongoing astrobiology program and complement each other: Curiosity focuses on past habitability, while Perseverance actively caches samples for a planned future return to Earth.
