# Risotto alla Milanese

Risotto alla Milanese is a classic saffron risotto from northern Italy.

**Ingredients (serves 4)**
- 320 g Carnaroli or Arborio rice
- 1 small yellow onion, finely diced
- 1 L hot chicken or vegetable stock
- 120 ml dry white wine
- A generous pinch of saffron threads
- 60 g butter (split), plus extra to finish
- 60 g finely grated Parmigiano Reggiano
- Salt

**Method**
1. Steep the saffron in a ladle of hot stock.
2. Sweat the onion in half the butter until translucent. Add the rice and toast, stirring, for 2 minutes.
3. Add the wine; let it evaporate.
4. Add hot stock a ladle at a time, stirring, waiting for each addition to absorb before adding the next. This should take about 18 minutes total.
5. Stir in the saffron stock partway through.
6. When the rice is al dente, remove from heat. Beat in the remaining butter and Parmigiano (the *mantecatura*). Rest 1 minute, then serve.
