# A Short History of Acme Robotics

Acme Robotics was founded in 2014 in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania by three former CMU graduate students. The company's initial focus was contract manipulation research for logistics customers.

In 2017 Acme raised its Series A and pivoted to building a commercial mobile manipulation platform. The first-generation R0 shipped to a handful of pilot customers in 2019 but was never sold at volume.

The Acme R1, launched in 2022, became the company's first widely-deployed product, with over 2,000 units shipped to customers in logistics, academic research, and light manufacturing.

Acme now has offices in Pittsburgh, Tokyo, and Berlin, and has grown to roughly 180 employees.
