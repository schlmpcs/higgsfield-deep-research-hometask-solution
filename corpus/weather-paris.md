# Climate of Paris

Paris has an oceanic climate (Köppen Cfb), strongly moderated by prevailing westerly winds from the Atlantic. Summers are mild to warm, winters are cool but rarely severe, and precipitation is distributed fairly evenly across the year.

- Average July high: ~25°C
- Average January low: ~3°C
- Annual precipitation: ~640 mm, spread across ~110 rainy days

Snow does occur in winter but rarely accumulates significantly. Summer heat waves have become more frequent in recent decades, with record highs above 40°C recorded in 2019 and 2022.
