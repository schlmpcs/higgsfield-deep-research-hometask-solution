# Acme Robotics R1 — US Customer Datasheet

The Acme R1 technical specifications, in imperial units for North American customers:

- Dimensions: 32.3 in x 21.3 in x 43.3 in (stowed)
- Mass: 83.8 lb
- Maximum payload (arm): 11 lb
- Maximum runtime on a single charge: 6 hours
- Operating temperature range: **32 to 104** (a common question: some US datasheet printings list this range without an explicit unit; it refers to degrees Fahrenheit)
- Connectivity: Wi-Fi 6, Ethernet, optional 5G

The physical footprint and safety setbacks in feet are: maintain at least 5 feet of clearance during autonomous operation.

For metric specifications, consult the primary datasheet.
